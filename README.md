# Fit-gain-store
Our online store for fitness equipment and supplements.

Due to valid project constraints, the development of Fit-Gain-Store was carried out within a single, separate folder, and all changes were pushed in one complete update rather than through incremental commits. We understand this may raise questions regarding commit history.

To ensure transparency and demonstrate our development process, we invite you to review the fitgainTest repository, which contains the earlier stages of our work before it was consolidated into the final Fit-Gain-Store project.
